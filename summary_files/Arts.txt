-The U.S. Constitution was ratified in 1788 and provides the foundation for the American government
-The Constitution establishes the three branches of government (legislative, executive, and judicial) and outlines their powers
-The Constitution also establishes the principles of federalism, which divide power between the national government and the states
-The Bill of Rights, which consists of the first 10 amendments to the Constitution, guarantees individual rights and liberties
-The five main food groups are fruits, vegetables, grains, protein, and dairy
-Fruits and vegetables are packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber
-Grains provide complex carbohydrates, essential for energy
-Protein is necessary for cell repair and growth
-Dairy is a good source of calcium, needed for strong bones and teeth
