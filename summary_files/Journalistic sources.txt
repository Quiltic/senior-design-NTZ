-The glass ceiling is a term that is used to describe the unseen, yet unbreakable barrier that keeps women from rising to the upper echelons of the corporate ladder.
-The term was first coined in the late 1970s and early 1980s.
-The glass ceiling is a result of various factors, including gender discrimination, stereotypes, and sexual harassment.
-Despite the progress that has been made in recent years, the glass ceiling remains a very real barrier for women in the workplace.
