-The Affordable Care Act (ACA) is a healthcare reform law that was passed in 2010.
-The ACA requires all Americans to have health insurance, and provides subsidies to help make coverage more affordable.
-The ACA also expands Medicaid, and requires all insurance plans to offer certain essential benefits.
-The ACA has been controversial, and has been the subject of multiple legal challenges.
-The ACA is still in effect, and has helped millions of Americans get health insurance.
