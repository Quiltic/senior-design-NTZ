-The American Revolution was a political and military conflict between the Kingdom of Great Britain and the thirteen colonies that became the United States of America
-The war began in 1775, and lasted until 1783
-The main causes of the war were the American colonists' desire for independence from British rule, and their resentment of British policies that they saw as unfair
-The war resulted in the defeat of the British and the establishment of the United States as an independent nation
-During the war, the American colonists were supported by France, Spain, and the Dutch Republic
