-The Amazon rainforest is a tropical forest that covers a large part of the Amazon Basin in South America.
-The Amazon rainforest is the world's largest tropical forest, with an area of 5.5 million square kilometers (2.1 million square miles).
-The Amazon rainforest is home to a diverse array of plants and animals, including many endangered species.
-The Amazon rainforest is under threat from human activities, including deforestation, logging, and mining.
