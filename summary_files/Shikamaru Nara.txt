-In the early universe, there was only one kind of particle: the quark.
-As the universe cooled, quarks began to bind together to form protons and neutrons.
-Protons and neutrons then began to bind together to form atoms.
-Atoms are the building blocks of all matter in the universe.
-The solar system consists of the sun and all the objects that orbit around it.
-The sun is the largest object in the solar system and is at the center.
-All the other objects in the solar system orbit around the sun.
-The solar system includes the eight planets and their moons, asteroids, comets, and meteoroids.
-The eight planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
-Mercury is the closest planet to the sun.
-Venus is the next closest planet to the sun.
-Earth is the third planet from the sun.
-Mars is the fourth planet from the sun.
-Jupiter is the fifth planet from the sun.
-Saturn is the sixth planet from the sun.
-Uranus is the seventh planet from the sun.
-Neptune is the eighth and last planet from the sun.
