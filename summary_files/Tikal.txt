-The scientific method is a systematic process for conducting scientific research.
-It is used to answer scientific questions and to test scientific hypotheses.
-The scientific method consists of four steps: 1. Observation and description of a phenomenon 2. Formulation of a hypothesis to explain the phenomenon 3. Conducting experiments to test the hypothesis 4. Analysis of the results of the experiments and drawing conclusions
-The scientific method is used by scientists all over the world to answer scientific questions and to test scientific hypotheses.
