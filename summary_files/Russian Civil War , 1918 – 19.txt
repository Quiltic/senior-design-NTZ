-The United States is a federal republic
-The federal government is made up of three branches: the executive, legislative, and judicial
-The executive branch is responsible for carrying out the laws
-The legislative branch is responsible for making the laws
-The judicial branch is responsible for interpreting the laws
-The federal government is divided into two parts: the national government and the state governments
-The national government is responsible for the country as a whole
-The state governments are responsible for the individual states
-The Constitution is the supreme law of the land
-In a competitive market, firms are price takers
-This means that each firm takes the prices as given and produces the quantity of output that maximizes its profits
-Firms in a competitive market are allocatively efficient because they produce the output that consumers desire most
-However, firms in a competitive market are not necessarily productive efficient
-This is because they may not be using the best technology or producing at the lowest possible cost
